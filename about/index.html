About

Hi, my name is Kevin. I initially studied Accounting as an undergrad then followed it up with an MSc in Logistics and Supply Chain Management. In my first job roles, I found myself doing several repetitive tasks, dealing with multiple data sources and data feeds. Reporting, analysing and operating day to day became problematic. Sometimes poorly implemented ERP/MRP systems or divergent business practices rendered existing systems incompetent at what should arguably be their core function. Automating tasks, centralising data and developing an analytical line of business tools quickly became a part of my skillset.

I am currently working on a Supply Chain and Operations Management library in python.
